PodClip
AI-powered podcast clip generator and publisher for creators.

PodClip is a tool for content creators that turns long-form YouTube podcast videos into short, vertical clips and publishes them to the creator's own TikTok account. It identifies the most engaging moments using AI, renders them into a vertical format, and lets each creator review and approve every clip before it is published.

What it does

  • Lets creators choose the podcast channels they want to source clips from
  • Transcribes the audio and uses AI to find the most engaging moments
  • Renders those moments into vertical clips suitable for short-form platforms
  • Lets the creator watch and approve each clip before it is published
  • Publishes approved clips to the creator's own connected TikTok account

How TikTok is used

Creators connect their own TikTok account to PodClip through TikTok's official login. PodClip then uses the Content Posting API to upload and publish the clips that the connected creator has reviewed and approved to that creator's own account. Each creator authorizes and controls their own connection, and can disconnect at any time.
