PodClip
AI-powered podcast clip generator for short-form video.

PodClip is a personal tool that helps a creator turn long-form YouTube podcast videos into short, vertical clips for social media. It identifies the most engaging moments from a podcast using AI, renders them into a vertical format, and lets the creator review each clip before publishing.

What it does

  • Pulls recent videos from podcast channels the creator selects
  • Transcribes the audio and uses AI to find the most engaging moments
  • Renders those moments into vertical clips suitable for short-form platforms
  • Lets the creator watch and approve each clip before it is posted
  • Posts approved clips to the creator's own TikTok account on their behalf

How TikTok is used

PodClip uses the TikTok Content Posting API solely to upload and post clips to the account of the authenticated creator who owns and operates this tool. It only ever posts content that the creator has personally reviewed and approved. It does not access, collect, or post on behalf of any other users.
