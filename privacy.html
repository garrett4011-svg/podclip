Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PodClip ("the Service") handles information. PodClip is a personal tool operated by a single creator for generating and publishing short-form video clips to their own social media accounts.

1. Information We Process

PodClip processes the following to function:

• Publicly available podcast videos that the operator chooses to process.
• Authentication tokens for social media accounts the operator connects (for example, a TikTok access token), used only to publish content to the operator's own account.
• Basic account information (such as the operator's TikTok username) needed to confirm the connected account.

2. How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to operate the Service: to generate clips and to publish clips that the operator has reviewed and approved to the operator's own connected accounts. Information is not used for advertising or sold to any third party.

3. Data Storage

The Service runs locally on the operator's own computer. Authentication tokens and generated clips are stored locally on that device and are not transmitted to any server other than the third-party APIs required to provide the Service (such as TikTok and the AI provider used to analyze content).

4. Third-Party Services

The Service uses third-party APIs, including TikTok and an AI content-analysis provider. Information shared with those providers is governed by their respective privacy policies. The Service shares only what is necessary to perform the requested actions.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties except as required to provide the Service's core functionality (for example, sending a video to TikTok to publish it) or as required by law.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Because data is stored locally on the operator's device, the operator may delete it at any time by removing the relevant local files. Connected account access can be revoked at any time through the respective platform's settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
